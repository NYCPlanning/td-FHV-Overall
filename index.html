For-hire Vehicle Trip Report

Overall Daily Trends

2019:
In 2019, the FHV trip volumes reached the peak point with an average daily trip count of 758,597. Peak Months: March (815,230) and April (786,657); Bottom Months: September (705,676) and August (707,483).

2020:
Overall in 2020, the FHV trip volumes decreased to an average daily trip count of 427,311 (56% of the 2019 average daily trip volumes). Since March 2020, the daily FHV trip volumes decreased as the Covid-19 hit the city. In April 2020, there were 159,367 FHV trips daily happening in the city, which only accounted for 20% of the pre-pandemic level (compared to April 2019). As the city entered the reopening periods, the average daily trip counts in October 2020 (465,584) has gradually increased to 63% of the pre-pandemic level (Compared to October 2019). However, as the Delta variant hit the city in the winter as the second wave, the FHV trip counts in December 2020 dropped to 409,905 which were 53% of those in December 2019.

2021:
In 2021, the FHV trip volumes increased to an average daily trip count of 517,195 (68% of the 2019 average daily trip volumes). The average daily trip counts in October 2021 (570,637) has gradually increased to 77% of the pre-pandemic level (Compared to October 2019).

2022:
In 2022, the FHV trip volumes increased to an average daily trip count of 620,199 (82% of the 2019 average daily trip volumes). The average daily trip counts in October 2022 (659,940) has gradually increased to 89% of the pre-pandemic level (Compared to October 2019).

2023:
The average daily trip counts in March 2023 (700,614) has gradually increased to 86% of the pre-pandemic level (Compared to March 2019).

FHV Trips by Hour

Clear AM peak, Pm peak, and midday patterns.

2020:

FHV Trips From/to CBD

FHV Trips from CBD to CBD Areas:
In 2019, the FHV trips picking up and dropping off within the CBD areas accounted for 16-19% of the overall FHV trips happening in the city. The percentages dropped to 4% during the month of April 2020 and climbed to 13% in October 2020. Until October 2021, it reached 17% of overall FHV trips and dropped back to 15% during the winter. In 2022, it remained 17% for most months.

FHV Trips from CBD to Non-CBD Areas:
In 2019, the FHV trips picking up within the CBD areas and dropping off outside the CDB areas accounted for 11-13% of the overall FHV trips happening in the city. The percentages dropped to 6% during the month of April 2020 and climbed to 9% in October 2020. Until October 2022, it reached 12% of overall FHV trips.

FHV Trips from Non-CBD to CBD Areas:
In 2019, the FHV trips picking up outside the CBD areas and dropping off inside the CDB areas accounted for 8-9% of the overall FHV trips happening in the city. The percentages dropped to 5% during the month of April 2020 and climbed to 8% in October 2020. Until October 2022, it reached 9% of overall FHV trips.

FHV Trips from Non-CBD to Non-CBD Areas:
In 2019, the FHV trips picking up and dropping off outside the CBD areas accounted for 59-64% of the overall FHV trips happening in the city. The percentages climbed to 85% during the month of April 2020 and dropped to 70% in October 2020. Until October 2022, it reached 62% of overall FHV trips.
